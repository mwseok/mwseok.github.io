About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Center for Climate and Carbon Cycle Research, Korea Institute of Science and Technology. I earned my Ph.D. in Environmental Science from Korea University, focusing on Atmospheric Deposition and Its Role in Nutrient and Carbon Cycling in the Global Ocean. Previously, I was a Visiting Scholar at Cornell University.
