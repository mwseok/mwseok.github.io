About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST), having earned my Ph.D. in Environmental Science from Korea University.

My research primarily focuses on marine biogeochemistry. I utilize computational tools and Earth System Models (like CESM2) to investigate the complex impacts of atmospheric aerosol deposition (such as N, P, Fe, and Silica) on ocean ecosystems and the global carbon cycle.
